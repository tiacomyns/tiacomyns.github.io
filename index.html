About Me

My introduction

Born in Hong Kong, raised in the Sydney beach suburb of Cronulla and currently residing in the beautiful South-East of Melbourne

I am a student at RMIT University studying a Bachelor of Information Technology with specialisations in Cyber Security and Design.
I am also currently working with the RMIT Centre for Digital Innovation on creating Mixed Reality Learning Experiences using the Microsoft Hololens 2!

As a RMIT Community Leader, Holding 2022 Executive Positions in two RMIT Affiliated Information Technology Clubs. The RMIT Programming Club (TPC) and the Society for Women in Information Technology (SWITCH).

I am passionate about promoting and uplifting Women in STEM fields and creating opportunities for all students to enrich their knowledge and create Networking opportunities between sponsor companies and our members.
